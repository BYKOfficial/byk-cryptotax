📋 Overview — The Short Version

Plain English summary: The calculator runs entirely in your browser. We don't see your numbers, we don't store them, and we don't sell them. The only time we receive any data is if you choose to email us a CSV file — and even then, we use it only to help you and delete it afterward.
🔒 No data stored 🚫 No ads or trackers 📧 Email optional only 🇮🇳 DPDP Act, 2023 aligned

📊 What Data We Collect

BYK CryptoTax collects minimal data. Here is a complete list:

  • Calculator inputs (purchase value, sale value, TDS): Processed entirely in your browser. Never transmitted to our servers.
  • Server logs: Standard web server logs may record your IP address and browser type when you visit the site. These are retained for up to 30 days for security purposes and are not used for profiling.
  • CSV files (optional): Only if you choose to email your exchange CSV for the bulk-help service. See section 4 for details.

We do not collect: names, email addresses (unless you contact us), Aadhaar numbers, PAN numbers, financial account details, or any personally identifiable information through the calculator.

🖥️ How the Calculator Works

The BYK CryptoTax calculator is a client-side tool. This means:

  • All calculations happen inside your web browser using JavaScript.
  • Your purchase value, sale value, TDS amount, and results are never sent to our servers.
  • We have no technical ability to see or access the numbers you enter.
  • Closing your browser tab clears all data — nothing is saved locally unless you explicitly choose to save or download a result.
Technical note: The calculator is a static React application served via Vercel's CDN. No backend server processes your financial inputs at any point.

📂 CSV Email Option

If you have a large number of transactions, we offer a manual service where you can email your exchange CSV file and receive a Schedule VDA summary in return.

  • This service is entirely optional — the calculator works without it.
  • CSV files sent to us are used only to prepare your tax summary.
  • We do not share, sell, or disclose your CSV data to any third party.
  • Files are deleted after your summary is sent to you.
  • By emailing your CSV, you consent to this processing.

🍪 Cookies

BYK CryptoTax uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Used to remember your cookie consent preference. No personal data.
  • No advertising cookies: We do not use Google Ads, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising network cookies.
  • No tracking cookies: We do not use cross-site tracking technologies.

Vercel, our hosting provider, may set technical cookies necessary for CDN delivery and security. These are governed by Vercel's Privacy Policy.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. The calculator will continue to work without cookies.

🇮🇳 DPDP Act, 2023 Compliance

BYK CryptoTax is designed to align with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act). Specifically:

  • Data Minimisation: We collect only what is necessary — and for the calculator, that is nothing at all.
  • Purpose Limitation: CSV data received via email is used only for preparing your tax summary and for no other purpose.
  • Storage Limitation: CSV files are deleted after your summary is delivered.
  • Transparency: This policy clearly describes all data practices.
  • Consent: Cookie consent is obtained before non-essential cookies are set.

As the DPDP Act framework continues to be notified by the Government of India, we will update our practices accordingly.

👶 Children's Privacy

BYK CryptoTax is intended for adult users who are filing income tax returns in India. We do not knowingly collect data from persons under 18 years of age. If you believe a minor has submitted data to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
